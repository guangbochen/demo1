THE MOTIVE CONTENT SPECIALISTS

Chicane Studios is a Production Agency creating inspirational and innovative content that captures audiences. Our experienced team is dedicated to producing all kinds of formats that are professional and entertaining.

conception

As a leading specialist in automotive and marine publishing, we have over 10 years experience producing content for many of the heavyweights of the automotive and marine industries and we love doing it. We apply our experience and values to help guide your creative concepts that connect your brand with your audiences.

production

We specialise in high impact and engaging video that conceptually and creatively aligns with your brand. Our flexibility means we can work with you from script to screen, or simply engage us at any stage of your project. Whatever the brief, we have the internal resources, access to Australia’s best automotive talent and a wealth of experience to deliver production solutions to suit you.

Distribution

With access to Australia’s largest automotive and marine influencer network, we can connect your content with your audience. Let us do the heavy work for you!

Digital platforms, CarAdvice and BoatAdvice, are the dominant leaders in social amplification within the motive arena. Chicane Studios can leverage its own and third party channels to amplify the reach of your content. Over years of developing and transforming our approach to social, we provide an optimised methodology to drive effective audience engagement.
